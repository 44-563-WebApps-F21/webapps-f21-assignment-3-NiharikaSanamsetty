Supergirl

She can fly and possesses super-strength (like Superman), she also has psychokinetic, shapeshifting, and cloaking/invisibility powers (the last makes her undetectable, even to Superman). The Matrix's Supergirl form resembles the Pre-Crisis Supergirl.
